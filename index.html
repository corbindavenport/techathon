The charity

Doctors Without Borders, also known as Médecins Sans Frontières, is a private international association founded in 1971. The association is made up mainly of doctors and health sector workers, with the mission to provide lifesaving medical care to those most in need.

The event

We're planning to do a 24-hour livestream featuring journalists, YouTubers, leakers, and other consumer tech enthusiasts you might already know. Expect games, chats with viewers, and much more! We're using the Tiltify platform for donations, so the money goes directly to DWB/MSF.
