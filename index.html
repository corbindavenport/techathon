Techathon is a charity livestream by the tech community, supporting healthcare access.

The charity

Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) cares for people affected by conflict, disease outbreaks, natural and human-made disasters, and exclusion from health care in more than 75 countries. That includes work in Gaza, with over 1,300 staff working in Gaza's hospitals, clinics, and other facilities

The event

We're planning a 12-hour livestream with bloggers, journalists, YouTubers, and other people in the tech community you might already know. Expect games, chats with viewers, and much more! We're using the Tiltify platform for donations, so all proceeds go directly to the organization.
