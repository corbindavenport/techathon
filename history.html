We've raised over $3,500 for charity to date.

August 2022

The August 2022 Techathon event was held two months after the United States Supreme Court decided that the right to abortion access should no longer exist. We raised $450 for the National Network of Abortion Funds, a group that helps remove financial and logistical barriers to abortion access. The 12-hour stream included discussion segments and playthroughs of Skribblio, Jackbox Party Pack, Team Fortress 2, and Deep Rock Galactic.

December 2021

The December 2021 event for Techathon raised $520.00 for Direct Relief, an organization that equips doctors and nurses with life-saving medical resources and helps organize emergency responses in over 80 countries and all 50 U.S. states. We had over 10 participants across two 12-hour streams, including a mix of discussion segments and games like Counter Strike: Global Offense, Valorant, and Jackbox Party Pack.

December 2020

The December 2020 event for Techathon raised $2820.00 for Doctors without Borders, an independent international medical humanitarian organization that provides aid to people in more than 70 countries. The event was a continuous 24-hour stream on Twitch, with discussion segments and games like Jackbox Party Pack, Dungeons and Dragons, and Minecraft.

Techathon is not directly affiliated with any of the charities, publications, companies, or other organizations metioned on this page.
